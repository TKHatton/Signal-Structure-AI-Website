How to Give Us Access

Step-by-step instructions for each platform we work in. No passwords. Just manager and collaborator invites.

Most clients finish this in under 10 minutes. We use manager invites and collaborator access wherever possible. You will never need to share a password with us. If anything below is confusing, email us before you get started.
Platform Access

Add us to each platform below

Our email for all invites: hello@signalstructure.ai
Only add us to the platforms you use. If you are not sure whether a platform applies to you, just ask.
Google Business Profile Manager invite
Why we need it: To update your listing, fix inconsistencies, and make sure your profile is complete and accurately read by AI platforms like Gemini and Google AI.
How to add us — 2 minutes
  1. Go to business.google.com and sign in
  2. Select your business profile
  3. Click the three-dot menu → Business Profile Settings → Managers
  4. Click Add, enter hello@signalstructure.ai, select the Manager role, and send the invite
We will accept the invite and get to work. When your engagement ends, remove us from that same Managers screen. Not sure where to find it? Search YouTube: "How to add a manager to Google Business Profile" for a short walkthrough.
Website / CMS Admin invite
Why we need it: To add or update structured data, fix technical issues, and update page content as part of your engagement.
Find your platform below
WordPress
Settings → Users → Add New → enter our email → Role: Administrator → Add New User
Squarespace
Settings → Permissions → Invite Contributor → enter our email → Role: Administrator
Wix
Settings → Roles and Permissions → Invite People → enter our email → Role: Admin
Webflow
Project Settings → Members → Invite Member → enter our email → Role: Designer or Admin
Shopify
Settings → Users and Permissions → Add Staff → enter our email → select relevant permissions
Different platform?
Email us at hello@signalstructure.ai and we will confirm the right method for your setup.
Not sure how to add someone? Search YouTube: "How to add admin user [your platform name]" for a short walkthrough video.
Google Search Console User invite
Why we need it: To check indexing status, submit your sitemap, and confirm your site is being read correctly by Google.
How to add us — 1 minute
  1. Go to search.google.com/search-console and sign in
  2. Select your property
  3. Click the gear icon → Users and Permissions → Add User
  4. Enter hello@signalstructure.ai, set Permission to Full, and click Add
Facebook / Instagram Page admin invite
Why we need it: To update business information in your profile that AI platforms read, if social is part of your engagement scope.
How to add us
  1. Go to business.facebook.com and sign in
  2. Go to Business Settings → People
  3. Click Add, enter hello@signalstructure.ai, and assign Admin access
  4. We will accept the invite from the notification email
Instagram access comes through your connected Facebook Business Manager. If your pages are not connected, email us first.
LinkedIn Company Page Page admin invite
Why we need it: To ensure your company profile is accurate and complete, since LinkedIn is a source AI platforms use to identify and describe businesses.
How to add us
  1. Go to your LinkedIn Company Page
  2. Click Admin tools (top right) → Manage admins
  3. Click Add admin and search for Lenise Kenney or enter hello@signalstructure.ai
  4. Select the admin role and save
